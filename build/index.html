ABOUT

Flaming Partners

developed the first Production Management System (PMS) with app support in the present Chinese production market. In the present production process, all the scheduling, list of props and customes and calculation of labour are all done traditionally by man-power which will use up unnesscessary time and resources, especially in printing materials. With the use of our PMS, we aim to improve the production workflow in communication, security and information sharing. Our system can safe time, man-power and most importantly the cost for production. It also achieve eco friendly purpose. We would like to emphasis our PMS is developed by experienced senior television producer and programmer. With this being said, it is almost impossible to be replicated or replaced by another system. Our strategy is to focus on our functionality and user friendly compatibility development in order to stay on top of the market.

APPLICATION

We are an online Production Management System [PMS]

with app support. Our service cover the entire process of production shoot from pre-production to post-production by project. We will create an individual secure project for each production. From creating the story, the script will enter into our system and it can automatically generate scene breakdown information for production use. We designed a central database store in cloud to save all information in a production. We provide realtime information upates and our system can send notifications to all production member at the same time. The information stored can be review anytime, anywhere will a computer or a moblie device. With our system, the production can significantly reduce cost in printing matierials because everything can be view online. PMS can lead the industry to become more enviromental friendly.
